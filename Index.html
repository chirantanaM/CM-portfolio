Professional Overview

Research professional specializing in BioNLP, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. With a PhD in Computer Science and Technology from IIEST, Shibpur, I focus on computational intelligence for biomedical article summarization and clinical information retrieval. Currently serving as Assistant Professor and Research Coordinator at the Centre for Data Science, JIS Institute of Advanced Studies & Research (JISIASR), JISU, West Bengal, India, I am passionate about advancing AI-driven solutions in healthcare and academia.
